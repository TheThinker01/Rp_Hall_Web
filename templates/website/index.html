Who We Are-The RPians

We live with creativity

We are the residents of the Rajendra Prasad Hall of Residence, known as RP Hall, one of the oldest halls of residence at Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur. It is named after Rajendra Prasad, the first president of the Republic of India. RP Hall is the second largest undergraduate hall of residence, in terms of size and boarder strength, and was the largest until LBS was converted into an undergraduate-only hall in 2014.
